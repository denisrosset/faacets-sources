List of Bell inequalities for at most 5 measurements per party via triangular elimination

There are 89 tight Bell inequalities involving at most 5 measurements per party that are generated by applying triangular elimination to the list [4] of facets of the cut polytope of the complete graph on n points with n≤9. They are used in the computational experiments described in [3]. They appear in the large list which is generated by a program available online from [2]. For how we obtain these Bell inequalities, see [1].

List: bell5.txt

The format of the list is described at this page [2].
